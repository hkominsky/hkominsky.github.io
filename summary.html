Summary and Additional Work

Summary

As college students, we face constant pressure in balancing work, physical well-being, and mental health, with nutrition being one of the most vital factors affecting our overall well-being. Proper nutrition plays a key role in enhancing the student's educational experience. With this in mind, we seek to examine the relationship between food quality ratings and factors such as tuition, geographical location, and student population, to uncover the key drivers of student satisfaction. The findings from this study will provide valuable insights into what schools are doing well and where they can improve, ensuring students make the most of their higher education experience.
